**Project Overview**

This project analyzes global merchandise trade transactions using Microsoft Excel to uncover sales performance insights across product lines, territories, and customers.

The objective was to transform raw transactional data into meaningful business intelligence that supports strategic decision-making.


**Business Objectives**

Identify top-performing product lines

Analyze regional sales distribution

Evaluate customer purchasing patterns

Support strategic decision-making

**Dataset Structure**

The dataset contains the following fields:

OrderId

OrderLineNumber

OrderedQuantity

ProductLine

ProductCode

MSRP

PriceEach

Sales

CustomerName

PhoneNumber

Territory

AddressLine1

AddressLine2

Country

CustomerCompany

Granularity:
1 row = 1 product per order (transaction-level data)


**Key Findings**

Classic Cars generated the highest revenue contribution.

Revenue consistently peaks in Q4 annually.

EMEA territories account for the majority of total sales.

Over 90% of orders were successfully shipped.

**Business Recommendations**

Increase inventory planning ahead of Q4 seasonal demand.

Focus marketing spend on high-performing product lines.

Strengthen customer retention strategies in lower-performing regions.

Monitor pricing strategy against MSRP for margin optimization.
